About Me

Full stack developer that relishes the challenges of both front and back-end. Independently leads projects to completion. Excels at helping others realize their product vision and personal development goals.

I really love coding and finding a solution when encountering difficult problems or bugs. Nothing is more satisfying in finding multiple solutions to hit a challenge. I love using the Technologies with React and Django, and how it simplifies the web developing process. Every time I learn new things from using these Libraries/Framework and every day I met a certain improvement in my coding skills.

Pursuing the path of becoming a Full-Stack Web-Developer is the most significant decision that had been ever made in my career. From college year to the first step of the competitive market, I overcome many majors and careers (ex. Mechanical Engineering, Finance, Accounting, Marketing, Sales), but none of them caught my attention. My final decision is made to pursue a web-developer because this is where I found out that this career is leading to my comfort zone. The challenge coming through web-developer is really interesting and solving a challenge is satisfying. It is not easy to know what is the best fit for me, but once web-developer caught my attention, I do not regret to move on to this path.
